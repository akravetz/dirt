# Decision: Reservoir Stand for Autopot FlexiTank Pro

**Date:** 2026-04-11
**Status:** Final

## What Was Decided

Elevate the 25-gallon FlexiTank Pro on an **oak wooden step stool, 6" height** (Amazon ASIN: B0F48D7YMW). Solid wood construction, originally designed as a bed step stool.

## Context

The Autopot system is gravity-fed — the reservoir must sit higher than the tray level for solution to flow. Setup details:
- Reservoir: Autopot FlexiTank Pro, 25-gallon (21"×21" base, ~208 lbs when full)
- Trays: floor-level (not elevated)
- Tubing runs: 8–10 feet to the furthest pot
- Location: bedroom closet — space is limited

## Why 6 Inches

Autopot's gravity-feed mechanism requires only minimal head pressure to overcome tubing friction over 8–10 foot runs at floor level. 6" provides sufficient pressure for this configuration. Additional height would increase flow rate and margin, but closet space constraints make a taller stand impractical.

**Monitoring plan:** Watch the furthest pot's tray during the first 48 hours after float valve activation. If the tray takes more than a couple of hours to refill after the plant has consumed it, elevation may be insufficient — add a second 6" riser under the stand.

## Alternatives Considered

| Option | Height | Reason Rejected |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Hongsane 22" plant caddy with wheels | ~2–3" | Too low for 8–10ft tubing runs |
| Cascade 22" heavy-duty plant caddy | ~2–3" | Right footprint dimensions but same height issue |
| Wire shelf rack | Variable | Would work but bulkier than needed for the closet |
| DIY plywood on cinder blocks | Variable | Effective but aesthetically poor |
| Oak step stool (chosen) | 6" | Right height, compact footprint, solid wood holds 208 lbs safely |

## Load Capacity Note

At 208 lbs full, verify the step stool's rated load before placing the filled reservoir. Solid wood construction at this category of bed step stool is typically rated for 300–400 lbs — confirm product listing before first fill.
