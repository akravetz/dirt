## ⚠️ Nutrient Solution EC — Action Required

User measured nutrient solution at **920 ppm (≈ EC 1.84)**. This is significantly above the early veg target of **EC 0.8–1.0** (400–500 ppm).

**EC 1.84 in early veg risks:**
- Nutrient burn (tip burn, leaf margin scorching)
- Salt accumulation in coco — especially problematic before float valve activation
- Compounding Plant C's existing issue if pH is also off

**Action:** Dilute the next feed. Mix a fresh batch of Canna A+B targeting EC 0.8–1.0. Use more water per the same nutrient volume, or reduce nutrient volume. Confirm with meter before watering.

**Conversion note:** TDS/ppm readings vary by meter factor. Most TDS-3 meters use a 0.5 conversion factor: ppm ÷ 500 = EC. At this factor, 920 ppm = EC 1.84. If using a 0.7 factor meter, 920 ppm ÷ 700 = EC 1.31 — still above target but less severe. Confirm which factor applies to your TDS-3.

## Float Valve Activation — Tomorrow Window Opens

Day 13 post-transplant (transplanted March 29). The **2–3 week post-transplant activation window begins April 12** (tomorrow). Do not activate based solely on the calendar — wait for behavioral readiness signals:

1. **Drinking faster** — finishing 150–200ml in under 24 hours
2. **Node development accelerating** — growth visibly picking up pace
3. **Upward-reaching foliage** — leaves angling toward light, turgid and vigorous

When all three are present, follow the activation procedure in [Autopot System](../concepts/autopot.md).
