## Issues & Flags

### 🔴 Afternoon VPD below floor (0.63 kPa)
VPD at 14:00 MDT is 0.63 kPa — below the 0.8 kPa veg floor. Root cause: tent temperature has cooled from yesterday's 76.01°F to 72.03°F, and with afternoon RH at 76.46%, the lower temp means less moisture-holding capacity, yielding a low VPD. This was explicitly flagged as a risk in the Apr 24 daily: "If afternoon temp dips below 74°F, elevated RH could push VPD below 0.8 kPa floor." Reducing humidifier output during lights-on is the direct fix; addressing the temperature drop is the underlying fix.

### ⚠️ Daytime temperature regression (72.03°F, below 74°F floor)
Daytime temperature dropped from yesterday's milestone (76.01°F) to 72.03°F at 14:00 and 71.8°F morning average — both below the 74°F day floor. Overnight temperature (69.2°F) remains within the 68–72°F veg night range. The cause is unclear from sensor data alone; possibilities include lower ambient room temperature, changed fan speed settings, or a passing weather system. The Apr 22 daily noted a similar pattern (temp dipped to 72°F) attributed to a weather system with a ~10 hPa pressure drop. Investigate before the lights-off window.

### ⚠️ Overnight RH slightly above veg target (56.68%)
Overnight RH averaged 56.68% — marginally above the 45–55% veg target ceiling. This is a slight upward trend from prior nights (Apr 22: 52.06%, Apr 24: 51.81%, Apr 25: 56.68%). If the trend continues, overnight VPD could drift below 1.0 kPa even with temperatures in range. The humidifier output reduction needed to fix the afternoon VPD will also help pull overnight RH down.

### ⚠️ Plants C and D moisture sustained very high
