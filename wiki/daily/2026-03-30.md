## Observations

Day 1 post-transplant. All 4 plants in Autopot XL pots. Observed ~⅛ inch runoff collecting in Autopot tray after hand-watering — a sign watering volume is too high for current root zone size.

**Runoff management decision:** Remove runoff from trays promptly using turkey baster, sponge, or tipping. Standing water creates anaerobic conditions before roots reach that zone.

**Watering adjusted:** Reduce from 250–500ml guideline to **~150–200ml per plant** targeted at stem base. Expand radius gradually as roots colonize the pot.

Plants appear upright and unstressed following transplant.
