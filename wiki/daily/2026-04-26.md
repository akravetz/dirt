## Issues & Flags

Cause: tent temperature (73.17°F) is running below the 74°F daytime target, limiting air's moisture-holding capacity; afternoon RH at 75.6% then yields a low saturation deficit. Reducing humidifier intensity during lights-on is the direct fix.

### ⚠️ All temperature windows below veg targets — second consecutive day

Overnight avg 67.85°F (target 68–72°F — 0.15°F below floor), morning avg 73.02°F (target 74–76°F), now 73.17°F (target 74–76°F). This mirrors Apr 25 exactly. The Apr 22 daily associated a similar dip with a weather system (~10 hPa pressure drop); if ambient conditions have remained cooler, reducing exhaust fan speed marginally during lights-on could recover the 1–2°F needed to reach target and simultaneously improve the afternoon VPD.

Overnight VPD (0.94 kPa) remains within the 0.8–1.2 target, but if overnight RH continues rising, overnight VPD will drift toward or below the floor. Reducing humidifier output during lights-on (to lower the afternoon dew point load) should cascade into improved overnight readings.
