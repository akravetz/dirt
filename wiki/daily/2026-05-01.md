## Flags

### Plant B root-zone moisture remains the top issue

B is still the wettest pot: 93.90% now, after 91.93% on Apr 29 and 86.74% on Apr 28. The photo still does not show visible wilt or canopy stress, but the trend is too wet for watch-and-wait. Keep B's float valve closed, or close it now if it was not done, and verify the tray/valve is not continuously feeding. Let the pot dry back toward roughly 70–75% before reopening unless manual inspection proves sensor error.

### C remains saturated; D is high but easing

C is holding around 90% and D is just under 87%. C has now been in the high-saturation zone for many days without obvious photo stress; D is slightly improved from the prior high group. Continue inspecting for droop after lights-on, sour media smell, stalled growth, and lower-leaf yellowing before deciding whether to restrict additional valves.

### Humidity/VPD control still needs attention

Overnight VPD again averaged 0.76 kPa, below the 0.8 kPa floor, while RH stayed near 67%. Daytime VPD is technically in band but near the wet edge (0.90 morning, 0.84 now), and RH climbed to 71.3% now. This points to too much moisture load and/or too little clearing for the current temperature. Review H7142 night behavior, RH ceiling/off-gate behavior, and exhaust/light heat settings before adding more mist.

### Temperature is improving but not fully fixed

Now temperature is back in the 74–76 deg F day target at 74.55 deg F, but morning averaged 73.75 deg F and overnight remained below the 68 deg F floor at 67.31 deg F. The day recovery is helpful; the overnight floor still matters because cool air pushes RH up and VPD down.

## Recommendations

1. **Keep Plant B drying back** — close/keep closed the B float valve, verify no tray feed-through, and reopen only after a clear dryback toward 70–75%.
2. **Do not add humidity** — H7142 control should bias drier until overnight VPD stays above 0.8 kPa and RH stops climbing into the high-60s/low-70s.
3. **Tuck A and D today** — bend the tallest sativa-leaning shoots laterally under the SCROG; let B/C continue growing upward into the net.
4. **Inspect C and D root zones** — C is still around 90%; D is easing but high. Intervene if either shows droop, sour smell, stalled growth, or spreading lower-leaf yellowing.
5. **Confirm clone + regular seed status** — the Apr 29 clone/germination task is overdue unless already completed; if not done, take the 8 cuttings and start all 10 SBxBS01 regulars now.
6. **Hold light/exhaust plan toward warmer, drier air** — maintain or complete the 50% light step if not already done, and avoid exhaust settings that pull overnight temperature below 68 deg F.
