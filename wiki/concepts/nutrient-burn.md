# Nutrient Burn

Nutrient burn ("nute burn") occurs when the nutrient concentration in the root zone is too high, causing the plant to take up excess salts. It manifests as leaf tip and edge damage and is one of the most common problems in coco coir grows.

## Symptoms

- **Leaf tip burn** — brown, crispy tips starting at the very tip of the leaf and progressing inward
- **Leaf edge burn** — brown crispy edges, especially on upper/newer leaves
- **Dark green leaves** — oversaturated green color (excess nitrogen)
- **Curling leaf tips** — tips curl downward ("the claw") in severe cases
- Typically appears on the **upper canopy first** (closest to light, highest transpiration rate)

## Causes

- **EC too high** — nutrient solution is too concentrated for the plant's stage
- **Salt buildup** — accumulated salts in coco from insufficient runoff
- **Foliar contact** — nutrient solution splashed directly on leaves (foliar burn, distinct from systemic burn)

## EC Targets by Stage (Canna Coco A+B)

| Stage | Target EC | Target ppm (500 scale) |
|-------|-----------|----------------------|
| Seedling | 0.4–0.6 | 200–300 |
| Early veg | 0.8–1.0 | 400–500 |
| Mid veg | 1.0–1.4 | 500–700 |
| Late veg | 1.2–1.6 | 600–800 |
| Flower | 1.4–1.8 | 700–900 |
| Late flower / flush | 0.0 | 0 |

## Diagnosis vs Other Issues

| Symptom | Nutrient Burn | pH Lockout | Light Burn |
|---------|--------------|------------|------------|
| Location | Tips/edges, upper canopy | Older/lower leaves | Top of canopy only |
| Pattern | Uniform brown tips | Spots, interveinal yellowing | Bleaching, yellowing |
| EC check | High | Normal or high | Normal |
| pH check | Normal | Out of range | Normal |

## Correction

1. **Measure EC** of nutrient solution and runoff
2. **Dilute** nutrient solution to target EC for current stage
3. **Flush** if runoff EC is significantly higher than input — water with half-strength or plain pH 5.8 water
4. **Increase runoff** — always water to 10–20% runoff in coco to prevent salt accumulation
5. Damaged leaf tips will not recover but new growth should be clean
