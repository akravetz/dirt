# Coco Coir Medium

## This Grow's Mix
**60% coco coir / 40% perlite** by volume.

- Primary coco: Canna Coco Professional Plus (pre-buffered). Local substitutes acceptable: Mother Earth Coco, FoxFarm Coco Loco, Botanicare CocoGro — must be pre-buffered, low EC, no added nutrients.
- Perlite: coarse horticultural perlite (any brand)

## Why Coco/Perlite

Chosen over living organic soil and DWC:
- Excellent air-to-water ratio (40% perlite = high oxygenation)
- Full control over nutrient delivery via Canna A+B
- Forgiving of mistakes vs. soil (faster nutrient response)
- Compatible with Autopot gravity-fed system
- Allows week-away periods with Autopot automation

## Key Properties

- **Inert medium:** Contains no nutrients — all nutrition comes from Canna A+B solution
- **Calcium/Magnesium:** Coco naturally binds Ca and Mg; Canna A+B is formulated to compensate — do NOT add supplemental CalMag unless deficiency symptoms appear
- **pH target:** 5.5–6.0 (slightly more acidic than soil's 6.0–7.0)
- **Pre-wetting:** Always pre-wet to pH 5.8 before planting

## Transplanting Notes
- Bury leggy/etiolated stems up to the first true leaves — cannabis roots from buried stem tissue
- Make a cube-shaped hole for the rapid rooter cube; backfill around it
- First watering: Canna A+B at EC 0.8–1.0, pH 5.8 after nutrients
