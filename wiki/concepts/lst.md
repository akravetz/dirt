# LST — Low Stress Training

LST is a training technique that bends and ties branches horizontally to open the canopy, expose bud sites, and keep height manageable — without cutting the plant.

## How It Works

1. Gently tie down main branches horizontally using soft plant ties or pipe cleaners
2. Anchor ties to the pot rim or stakes in the coco
3. Goal: expose the lower canopy to light; create multiple bud sites at roughly equal height
4. Adjust ties every few days as the plant grows toward the light

## When to Apply (This Grow)

- **Start:** Can begin now (plants approaching readiness as of Day 19, Apr 2)
- **Priority plants:** A and D (primary keeper candidates)
- **Duration:** Continue through veg; stop tucking ~2–3 weeks into flower once buds form
- **After topping:** Wait 5–7 days for recovery, then resume LST on the two resulting main shoots

## In the Training Sequence

This grow uses: **Single Top → LST → SCROG**

LST bridges topping and the SCROG net. After the single top at node 4–5 creates two main shoots, LST spreads them horizontally until the SCROG net is installed (weeks 6–8 of veg).

## Key Notes
- No further topping after the single top — keep it simple
- Cannabis responds well to bending; stems become woody and hold position over time
- Low risk of damage if bends are gentle and incremental
